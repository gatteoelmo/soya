Nice to meet you!

I'm Matteo Gallardo , but everyone calls me Soya! I add flavor to everything I see or do, adding colors, creativity, and of course, tastiness! Sbam!

Originally from Torino, Italia, I come from a typical terrestrial family with a passion for technology. By the age of 6, I knew how to use a camera and enjoyed playing games on my GAME BOY (not that I condone piracy). Although I studied architecture at the Politecnico di Torino, I never pursued a career as an architect, apart from internships and workshops in Italy and Spain. For the past two years, I have been living in Santa Cruz de Tenerife, working as a freelance graphic illustrator and 3D modeler while also studying to become a web developer and web designer.

.process

1
BREAFING

Drop me a detailed brief with who you are, what you stand for, your business aims, who you're targeting, what you're selling, must-have features, project scope (or a rough layout), when you need it done, any websites you're into, and anything else you reckon is key.

2
EVALUATION

I will take a close look at your brief and assess whether the project meets the terms and requirements of both parties. If there is a match, we will have a online meeting to discuss how to proceed.

3
KICK-OFF

We'll brainstorm ideas and hash out concepts to find the ideal solution for your needs. We can have multiple sessions if needed to nail it down just right.

4
DESIGN

Here's where the magic starts to unfold. I'll dive into art direction, concepts, and design. In the initial phase, I'll share a mood board or a few screens to capture the vibe and aesthetics of the end product. Once we've locked in the direction, I'll flesh out the entire project with my designs.

5
DEVELOPMENT

Once the design or mood board gets the green light, I'll dive into developing the respective screens. Throughout the process, I'll keep you in the loop with regular updates on the development status, ensuring you always know where things stand.

6
QUALITY ASSURANCE

Once development wraps up, we'll put the site through rigorous testing on various browsers and devices to iron out any bugs. After your final thumbs-up, the site is good to go live!
