Meet Matteo Gallardo

A dude with a free spirit and a contagious smile that brings a breath of fresh air to the world of design.
Hailing from vibrant Torino, Matteo grew up amidst the bustling streets of the city, nurturing his passion for art and architecture since childhood. After successfully completing his master's degree in architecture at the Politecnico di Torino, he began making his mark in the professional world with his infectious energy and overwhelming creativity. His adventures have taken him to incredible places, from the offices of an engineering firm in Torino, where he contributed to the design of a nursing home, to the sunny beaches of Tenerife, where he discovered a newfound passion for digital illustration and 3D modeling. With his backpack on his shoulders and his adventurous spirit, Matteo has shown no fear in pushing boundaries and embracing the opportunities life presents.

In addition to his serious work, Matteo has a playful side that shines through in his artistic talent and his ability to transform abstract concepts into digital artworks that captivate the imagination of all who see them. With his infectious humor and lively personality, Matteo is the kind of person everyone loves to work with.
Today, Matteo is exploring new horizons in the world of web development, bringing with him his passion for design and his boundless curiosity. With his unique blend of technical skills and creativity, he is ready to make a difference wherever he goes.
With Matteo Gallardo on your team, work becomes an adventure and every project an opportunity to create something extraordinary.
